Wedding Cake Boxes

We have hundreds of products to beautifully package your smaller goodies, in a range of colours to suit your individual needs. Here at Samanal Card Center, we believe that a box is just a box without creative input. That's why we pride ourselves on creating cake packaging that looks great and will also keep your cake safe. Something we also take seriously here at Packqueen is the environment. We know that cake packaging serves one purpose; to protect your cake - it's there to safely transport your cake from one place to another, or to display it nicely at an event. Once it's used, it'll be thrown away. That's why we only stock biodegradable cake packaging. Rather than sitting in landfill for years to come, our packaging will gradually decompose into natural materials.
